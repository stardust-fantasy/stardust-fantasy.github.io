Burnout Multi-Media Universe

Burnout is a collaborative project that aims to bring together artists in order to create an expansive story and setting



[Kill with Kindness] Written by Cosmic Smoothie

Something sinister is happening at a daycare. A woman named Jane wonders who could be responsible.

[The Awakening: A Stoner Story] Written by Stoner Sudowoodo

An undying addict is poisoned unnaturally. He seeks out the supplier in order to save the lives of other people and uncovers something disturbing.
